The Tiger

The people under the Chinese Zodiac animal ‘Tiger’ are; brave, courageous and aggressive making them very respected. They excel at expressing themselves and are easily able to win others’ trust. They are often short-tempered and do not think long term with their actions. Sometimes over confident and traitorous making them unable to cooperation with others. Horse and dog are their best match.


Years of the tiger: 1950, 1962, 1974, 1986, 1998, 2010

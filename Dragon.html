The Dragon

People under the sign of the dragon are energetic, friendly and good listeners. They often strive for perfection and are very sensitive about their reputation. Can be a little arrogant and impatient at times. They cannot control their mood very well due to being eccentric and intolerant. They do not lack romance in their lives but it might take some time before they find their true love. People born in the year of the dragon are compatible with rat and monkey.


Years of the dragon: 1952, 1964, 1976, 1988, 2000, 2012

The Monkey

Those born in the year of the monkey are entertaining, versatile and enthusiastic. They are innovative with their actions and are great problem solvers. Willing to help others and strong desire for knowledge is what makes them incredibly flexible. They enjoy presenting themselves when communicating and are not easily controlled. Sometimes they can be selfish, arrogant and also jealous of others. They should be compatible with rat and snake but should avoid tiger and pig.

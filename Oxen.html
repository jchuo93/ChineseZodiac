The Oxen

Strengths for people born in the year of the oxen include: being reliable, determined and strong devotion to everything they do. They are not easily affected by those around them and know right from wrong. Communication is not usually their strong point, they are more likely to do what they think is correct instead of listening to others. They are compatible with rat, snake and rooster and should avoid horse, dog and sheep.


Years of the oxen: 1949, 1961, 1973, 1985, 1997, 2009


Famous individuals born in the year of the oxen:
Napoleon Bonaparte, Adolf Hitler, Barack Obama, Richard Nixon, Vincent Van Gogh
